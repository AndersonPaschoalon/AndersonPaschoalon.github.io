My name is Anderson dos Santos Agondi Paschoalon. I’m a Junior developer for HST since 2018.

I have a Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering with certification in Computing Engineering at State University of Campinas in 2014. I worked as a treinee at CPQD from 2013 to 2014, in the LTE/4G project. I'm graduated (M.S.) in Computer Engineering on Computer Networks in the same university in 2019.

My professional skills and interests are focused on C/C++, Linux and Hardware development. I'm also interested in Machine Learning and Neural Networks, game development.

My inspiration on building my career was understanding how electronic devices worked on its core, and affect other peoples lives with my skill. I enjoy using my skills to learn how to control different kinds of devices. Using my skills I contributed to the development of a made in Brazil 4G technology, with advances on computer network Science with a publication the "IEEE Networking Letters" scientific magazine, and now with smart card technologies at HST.

In my free time I like to hang out with my wife Rubia, I like to play video games (and use my skills to develop mods on them), and get in touch with nature working in my orchard, on trails and travelling.
